Democratic Techno-Socio-Capitalism

A Governance Framework for the Automated Age

Democratic Techno-Socio-Capitalism addresses economic distribution, data privacy, and legal enforcement in technologically advanced societies.

Economic System

Universal Basic Income funded by automation taxation, universal healthcare, robust worker protections, and competitive markets with strong antitrust enforcement.

→ Details

Healthcare System

Universal healthcare coverage ensuring medical care as a fundamental right, decoupled from employment and wealth.

→ Details

Housing System

Progressive property taxation that increases with portfolio size, discouraging accumulation while maintaining owner-occupied housing affordability.

→ Details

Privacy & Data Rights

Zero-knowledge identity verification and siloed data architecture. Citizens own their data; verification occurs without revealing personal information.
