Democratic Techno-Socio-Capitalism

A Governance Framework for the Automated Age

Democratic Techno-Socio-Capitalism addresses economic distribution, data privacy, and legal enforcement in technologically advanced societies.

Economic System

Universal Basic Income funded by automation taxation, universal healthcare, robust worker protections, and competitive markets with strong antitrust enforcement.

Healthcare System
